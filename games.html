Minecraft

This game is an open world, sandbox game that generates random servers. This game features a survival mode, where you need to mine for materials, build shelters, and fight off monsters that come at night. It also has Creative mode, where the player is invincible and can have access to different types of materials for building or for combat.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:
  • This was the first ever game that I tried.
  • Its a cool way to test your creativity.
  • I can be an architect without actually being an architect (by designing/building houses).
  • I can never grow out of it.
  • You can also play with friends online.
